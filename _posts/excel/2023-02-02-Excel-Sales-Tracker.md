## Prompt

  <div class='promptinnerdivtop'>
    <div class='prompttextdiv'>
    <p>Create a sales tracker using an Excel spreadsheet to keep track of all sales, customers, and revenue for a small business.</p>
    </div>
  </div>


## Example answer
  <div class='promptinnerdivbottom' >
    <div class='prompttextdiv'>
    <p>

<ul>
    <li>Create a spreadsheet with the following columns: Date, Customer Name, Items Sold, Quantity, Unit Cost, Total Cost, Revenue</li>
    <li>Enter all sales data into the appropriate columns.</li>
    <li>Add formulas to calculate Total Cost and Revenue (Total Cost = Quantity x Unit Cost; Revenue = Quantity x Unit Cost - Total Cost)</li>
    <li>Create charts/graphs to display customer-based revenue and overall revenue.</li>
    <li>Create a summary table that includes total revenue for each customer and an overall total.</li>
</ul></p>
    </div>
  </div>
